Project Idea

Overview

My project will be a sandbox computer game with a high degree of freedom, in which players can use the resources in the game to create and modify anything they want. Players will enjoy exploring unknown areas randomly generated by the system, and use the rewards obtained from exploration to build their own homes. It's a bit like "Minecraft", but the artistic style will be more realistic. Just like most ordinary PC games, players can use the keyboard, mouse and gamepad to play. In addition, this game will be playable using VR devices.

Motivation

Sandbox games are currently one of the most attractive games. "The Legend of Zelda", "Minecraft" and "Ark Evolved Survival" are now very popular sandbox games, especially "Minecraft", which has sold more than 176 million copies and it is the best-selling game of all time, many people enjoy this type of game. This type of game is not like those games that set tasks to be completed by the player. In this type of game, players can set their own goals to complete, such as building a house or collecting some items. They let players use their imagination, which is why they are attractive. This project will contain many different planets, each with a different style of environment and creatures. For players, this will be a brand new attractive world.

Description

At the beginning of the game, the player will be randomly born in the wild of the first planet with a certain hit point and a set of values representing the player's ability. Over time, if the player does not eat food, the player's hunger level will decrease. When it drops to a certain level, it will affect the player's mobility and even death. At the same time, there may be some creatures that will attack and kill the player. Therefore, in order to survive, players should try to find food and shelter. In order to better obtain food and shelter, players may need some tools. They can collect some rattans and sticks from plants in the game to make necessary tools. By using this tool, players can easily obtain more useful resources, such as stone and wood, so that they can build their own shelter. For long-term survival, players can also grow some crops or feed some edible creatures to obtain a stable source of food. When players have enough resources, they can explore their planet. The first planet will restore the earth’s environment as much as possible. There will be many different landforms in the first planet such as desert, forest, ocean, volcano.

However, this is not the all of the game. In addition to exploring on the planet, players can also develop technology. Players can build laboratories and workshops to develop the technology tree owned by players. The higher the level of these buildings, the more complex things players can create, such as vehicles, computers and even spacecraft. When the player makes a spacecraft that can leave the first planet, the second stage of the game begins. Players can control the spacecraft to go to any place before the spacecraft runs out of fuel. When they reach another planet, they will discover a completely different world. It may be a desert planet with stone and sand, or a planet full of sea water, or a strange planet with all kinds of strange creatures and even dangerous monsters. Players can start new adventures on those planets. As players explore further, they will obtain a variety of new resources, which will further develop their technology tree. As a result, they will be able to create advanced technology products, such as robots that can follow player commands, portals that can teleport anything to a set location, and even Dyson balls that can get energy from stars. Future technologies that only exist in sci-fi movies will be present in this game.

In addition, this game is also a multiplayer online game. Players can invite their friends to play together on their planet. At the same time, players can also set up their own servers and play games with other strangers. In the case, players can work together or fight with each other. This will be a different experience than playing alone.

Tools and Technologies

To build the basic framework of the game, I need to use Unity3D. I need to use C# language and very complex algorithms to generate random maps, I need so Visual Studio 2019. In order to achieve a real physics system, I will use Havok as the physics engine for this game. Many 3D models are needed, so Blender will be the tool for building them. In order to realize the multiplayer game mode, I also need a powerful server to provide players with a good online experience.

Skills Required

This project need a lot of skills, it should be done by a team. First of all, I need to be proficient in the use of unity3d and C# language to ensure that the game can be built. Secondly, the use of Havok physical system and a lot of algorithms are required. Then, I need to be proficient in using blender to model and write a lot of motion data. Server installation and management are also necessary.Mastering all these skills is a huge challenge, and it takes a lot of work to master them, but it is still feasible.

Outcome

If this project is successful, then this may be the richest sandbox game ever. However, the difficulty of realizing this project is very high. The initial problem may be insufficient funds. Therefore, this project requires high investment to realize. If everything can be solved, and this game can enter the field of vision of players, then it will be highly discussed on the Internet. When playing this game, players should experience an unprecedented feeling, and even trigger thinking about the relationship between man and the universe.
